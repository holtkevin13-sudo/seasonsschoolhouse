Flagler County, FL

Passionate about hands-on learning, community, and the freedom to educate with intention.

Meet the Founder

I’m a wife and mom who began our homeschool journey in 2024 — and it changed everything. Stepping away from the conventional path was both freeing and humbling, and it opened my eyes to just how many other families were walking the same road, often quietly and often alone.

I’ve always carried a deep passion for childhood education — the belief that children learn best when they’re engaged, curious, and doing. That belief became the seed for everything that followed.

How It Began

Seasons Schoolhouse began with a simple desire — to find something better for my own child when we first stepped into homeschooling.

Like many families, I was looking for more than just a place to meet a couple of times a month. I wanted a community where learning felt alive, where children could grow together, and where families weren’t just passing through each other’s lives — but truly building something alongside one another.

Over time, that desire grew into a clearer vision: a place where families could do life and learning together through all of its seasons. Not just the academic seasons of education, but the real seasons of childhood, growth, curiosity, and change.

What has grown from that original desire is a community rooted in connection. Not rushed, not transactional — but steady, familiar, and meaningful over time.

Welcome to Seasons Schoolhouse — a homeschool enrichment community rooted in experience, nature, and meaningful connection. We are so glad you are here.
