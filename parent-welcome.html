The Shape of the Year

What the Year Holds

Our year unfolds in a steady, unhurried rhythm — bi-weekly gatherings, themed field trips, and celebrations that mark the seasons of learning together. Here is the shape of what's ahead.

19 Gatherings
6 Paid Field Trips
3 Celebrations

Teaching Gatherings

Themed sessions where we dig deep into an essential theme together, using books, hands-on projects, local parks, and nature's own teachers. Every gathering is around three hours of learning, playing, and being together.

Field Trips

Six trips take us further afield — to museums, nature preserves, historical sites, and working farms. We balance paid destinations like Silver Springs, Timucuan, and St. Augustine with free local outings that let us explore Florida on a shoestring.

Parties & Celebrations

Three celebrations across the year — a kickoff social, a mid-year gathering, and a year-end celebration. These are for the kids, for the friendships, and for marking the seasons of learning together.

Scholarship Reimbursement

STEP UP
For Students

Seasons Schoolhouse LLC is working toward becoming a direct provider for Step Up For Students.

Only if this applies to you. This step is for families participating in the Florida Scholarship (Step Up For Students). If that's not you, you're done with the checklist — skip ahead to Stay Connected for what comes next.

If your family participates in Step Up For Students, Seasons Schoolhouse tuition may be reimbursable. We're working toward becoming a direct provider. While that's in progress, here's how reimbursement works today:

  1. After you complete the Family Form, you'll receive an invoice by email from Seasons Schoolhouse LLC.
  2. Pay the invoice. You'll receive a receipt by email once payment is processed.
  3. Log in to your Step Up For Students account and open the EMA portal (Education Market Assistant). Go to the Reimbursements section and start a new request.
  4. Upload your Seasons Schoolhouse invoice and proof of payment (receipt) as PNG, JPG, or PDF. Enter the provider name, student, service dates, and amount as prompted.
  5. Step Up reviews and, if approved, reimburses eligible families within roughly 2–4 weeks. Keep your receipt for your records.
What to have ready
  • Your Seasons Schoolhouse invoice (emailed after your Family Form)
  • Your Seasons Schoolhouse receipt (emailed after payment)
  • Your Step Up For Students / EMA account login
  • Your student's details as they appear on your scholarship account

Policy and timelines can change. For the most current Step Up reimbursement policy and portal access, please visit stepupforstudents.org, or email danyelle@seasonsschoolhouse.org if you'd like help walking through the process.
