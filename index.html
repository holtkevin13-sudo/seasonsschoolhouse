Homeschool Enrichment Program · Est. 2024 · Flagler County, FL

Seasons
Schoolhouse

A place to gather, explore, and grow — together.

A bi-weekly enrichment program for homeschool families in Flagler County. We are not a school. We are a community — built on shared values, real experiences, and the steady rhythm of showing up for one another.
